TIPS & TRICKS

Here are my SUPER SECRET tips for training Ghost and Psychic Pokemon to be the STRONGEST in the game!!!

HOW TO GET GENGAR:

  1. Catch a Gastly in Sprout Tower at night
  2. Train it until level 25 when it evolves to Haunter
  3. Trade it with a friend and have them trade it back
  4. BOOM! You have a Gengar!

BEST MOVES FOR PSYCHIC TYPES:

  • Psychic - BEST Psychic move in the game
  • Recover - Heal yourself in battle!
  • Reflect - Reduce damage from physical attacks
  • Elemental Punches - Teach your Alakazam Fire/Ice/Thunder Punch!

HOW TO BEAT ELITE FOUR WITH GHOST/PSYCHIC TEAM:

I beat the Elite Four with JUST my Ghost and Psychic Pokemon! Here's how:

  • Will (Psychic): Use your Ghost Pokemon and Dark moves
  • Koga (Poison): Psychic moves DESTROY his team
  • Bruno (Fighting): Psychic types have advantage
  • Karen (Dark): Use Alakazam with Fighting moves
  • Lance (Dragon): Gengar with Ice Punch works great!
